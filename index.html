Lesson 1

What is Puffer Advantage?

Think of puffer advantage as a measurable edge gained when a puffer-like strategy shifts expected outcomes in your favor.

Core idea: Advantage = Expected Gain with strategy ‚àí Expected Gain without strategy

Quick check

If your expected gain rises from 0.42 to 0.57, your puffer advantage is 0.15.

Decision rules & edge cases

  • Advantage flips under small parameter shifts ‚Üí treat as fragile.
